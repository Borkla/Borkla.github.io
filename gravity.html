Gravity

My role and the project in general

Gravity is a 3D platformer game where you bend your own gravity to navigate a space station The game is made in Unity and coded entirely in C# A big thing in this project for me was to be consistent with summarys and comments in our scripts to increase the readability for the rest of the team

During this project we where two programmers that made all the functionality for the game wich included our own collision physics, character controller and more.

What i learned during this project was that i got to really learn the value of readability in your code, i also got my first experience in coding state machines.
